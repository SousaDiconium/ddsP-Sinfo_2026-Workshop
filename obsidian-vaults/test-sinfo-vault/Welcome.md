# Welcome to SINFO!

SINFO is Portugal's largest free technology conference, connecting students, professionals, startups, and global innovators. Every year, we host a week-long event at the Técnico Innovation Center in Lisbon, bringing together the brightest minds in computer science and technology.

## Why Attend?
- **Speakers:** Hear from international visionaries and industry leaders.
- **Panels:** Join discussions on the latest tech trends.
- **Networking:** Meet peers, experts, and companies.
- **Workshops:** Participate in hands-on sessions to master new skills.
- **Company Presentations:** Discover opportunities and innovations.
- **Giveaways:** Enjoy exclusive prizes and swag!

## SINFO 33
This year's edition takes place from **20th to 24th April 2026** at the Técnico Innovation Center, Lisbon. All activities are completely free!

## Our Mission
SINFO is a student-led nonprofit dedicated to connecting the tech community, fostering learning, and unlocking opportunities for everyone.
