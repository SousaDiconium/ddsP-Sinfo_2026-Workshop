### Objectives

#### Bologna Degree in Computer Science and Engineering - Alameda

To provide understanding of fundamental concepts related to programming, namely, algorithm, procedural abstraction and data abstraction, programming and construction of abstractions, programming paradigms. After completing this course, students should master the concepts presented and be able to develop programs in a high-level programming language, Python.
